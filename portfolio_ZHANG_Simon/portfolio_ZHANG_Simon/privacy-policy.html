Protection of your personal data

1. Data Controller

Simon Zhang

2. Data Collected

When you use the contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message

Legal basis: Explicit consent via the form checkbox.

3. Purpose of Processing

The collected data is used solely for:

  • Responding to your contact requests
  • Exchanging regarding professional opportunities (internships, jobs)

No data is used for commercial or advertising purposes or shared with third parties.

4. Cookies

This site uses no tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies.

A technical cookie may be used to remember your consent to this privacy policy. This cookie contains no personal data and enables no tracking.

5. Data Retention

Contact form data is retained for a maximum of 12 months, then automatically deleted.

If you wish for earlier deletion, you can exercise your right to erasure by contacting me.

6. Your GDPR Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: obtain a copy of your personal data
  • Right of rectification: correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: request deletion of your data
  • Right to object: object to the processing of your data
  • Right to data portability: retrieve your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Right to restriction: request limitation of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact me at: contact@simonzhang.fr

I commit to responding to your request within a maximum of 1 month.

7. Data Security

Your data is protected by appropriate security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption: all communications are secured
  • Restricted access: only the site owner has access to the data
  • No sharing: no data is sold or shared with third parties
  • Secure hosting: servers compliant with security standards

8. Data Transfer Outside the EU

Data collected via this site is hosted within the European Union and is not transferred to third countries.
